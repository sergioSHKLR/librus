# Reader guide

## Brand

**L.I.B.R.U.S** — **annotate / read / consult**

## Wide (desktop)

Three panes (librus-nano model):

1. **Annotate** — Hypothesis (notes host)  
2. **Read** — book, TOC, search  
3. **Consult** — context iframe (Luz, Wiki, Wiktionary; places via Wiki)  

Provider links in the text open the consult pane.

## Narrow (tablet / phone)

- **Main book only** + **Hypothesis standard sidebar** (no custom container)  
- **No consult pane**  
- **Zero provider links** in the text (research is desktop-only)  
- Fullscreen notice: limited experience; **Dismiss** stored in `localStorage`

Mobile is for consulting your notes and reading — not for research.

## Notes

Log in via Hypothes.is. Export/backup notes in your Hypothesis account, not in app settings.
