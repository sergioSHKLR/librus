## What this is {#demo-about}

This is a **generic sample book** for trying the three-pane reader without needing a full novel.

On a **wide screen** (‚â• ~1200px):

1. **Annotate** on the left (Hypothes.is)
2. **Read** in the centre
3. **Consult** on the right ‚Äî Wikipedia, Wiktionary, or media embeds

On a **narrow screen** you get the book and notes only (no research links).

Open **Display** (sliders icon, top right) for:

- **Link density** ‚Äî None ¬∑ Some ¬∑ All (lowest = zero research links)
- **Providers** ‚Äî Wikipedia / Wiktionary
- **Typography** ‚Äî text size, line height, justify, column width
