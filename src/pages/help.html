Help

Quick start for the three-pane reader.

Start

  1. Open a book from the library.
  2. Read in the center; annotate with Hypothesis on the left (wide) or standard sidebar (narrow).
  3. On a wide screen, select a term or click a link to consult on the right.
  4. Use Contents (list icon) to jump sections; search in the main toolbar.
  5. On wide screens, open Links (density chip) for LO/MED/HI and providers L/W/D.
  6. Theme and full settings live on the library home — reader keeps text size only.

Panes

  • Annotate — Hypothesis notes and highlights.
  • Read — book text, search, contents.
  • Consult — Luz Espírita, Wikipedia, Wiktionary (wide only).

Mobile

Narrow screens show the book and notes only — no research links. Prefer landscape desktop for the full experience.

Notes

Log in via Hypothes.is. Export notes from your Hypothesis account. App settings export only preferences (not annotations).
